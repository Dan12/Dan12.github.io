General

Hello, my name is Daniel Weber. I am currently in 11th grade. I really enjoy math (currently in calculus) and science (currently in Engineering and Physics) at my high school. I actively take part in my high school's engineering club and participated in Square One's Innovative Vehicle Design Challenge. During the summer of 2013, I took an HTML and CSS intensive class through the Northwestern CTD. I have also taken an introduction to Java class through CTD and hope to further my programming knowledge and engineering skills throughout my life. I also enjoy playing soccer for my high school and doing Tae Kwon Do.

Hobbies
