Can you infect the world? You must bring about the end of human history by evolving a deadly, global Plague whilst adapting against everything humanity can do to defend itself. Plague Inc. evolves the strategy genre and pushes mobile gaming (and you) to new levels. Itâ€™s You vs. the world - only the strongest can survive!

This game is a great puzzle game and will really push your thinking and responsive skills to the limit. Even though this is the point of the game and it would be boring if it was easier, it has a hard time integrating itself in this modern world of smartphone gaming. It is a fun game with tons of different ways to play and interact with the game, but there is also a line where difficulty becomes to great for many and people are either forced to buy the full game to make it a little easier or they quite the game alltogether. On the up side, it has a continuosly changing and adapting game environment and several different plauge types and difficulties to chose from. All in all, a fun but sometimes frusterating game. Rating: 3.8/5
