Fruit Ninja

Fruit Ninja is a juicy action game with squishy, splatty and satisfying fruit carnage! Become the ultimate bringer of sweet, tasty destruction with every single slash! Fruit Ninja is the original and the best slasher on Android. The addictive gameplay will keep you coming back for even higher scores!

This classic game fully utilisizes the touch fatures of todays smartphones and allows for endless fun and slashing. You can sit there for hours and slash away at fruit and breath sighs of releif when you miss a bomb. You can compete with your friends and see who can get the highest score. It does become a little repetitive after a short time playing it. The three different modes add a little variety and the new store lets you get better scores, but it does become boring. However, it is very well made and totally draws the user in. Also, another drawback is that some achievements are slightly unrealistic and the free version does come at the cost of having a less variable gaming environment. Overall, it is a fun and enjoyable game for beginners, and is nice to play when you have some free time. Rating: 4.0/5
